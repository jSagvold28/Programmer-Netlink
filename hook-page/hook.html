Why Programmer Netlink?

Supporting Indie Development

This entire website is made entirely by one person. And of course tons of StackOverflow, GitHub, and ChatGPT solutions to help debug this. Seriously, don't believe me check the contributors on this project. Oh yeah, did I mention that this entire project is open-source on GitHub?

100% Open-Source

This whole project is open source on GitHub search Programmer Netlink and make sure its from jSagvold28. This is to ensure that your privacy is secured and you can learn from this project, like I am! Hope you learn a thing or two there! Or maybe you could give it a star!

Privacy Orentated

Programmer Netlink respects your privacy. So we believe that your privacy should be valued, because I want mine to be. Therefore, Programmer Netlink does not sell any of your data. All of the data is saved locally on your device, not on servers. We store data in Local Storage which is a storage method in JavaScript.

Programmer Exclusive

Programmer Netlink is a programmer social media app. None of those TikTok dancers here, nor fake conspiracy theories. Just your fellow programmers talking about code, bugs, or anything coding related.

Unleash Programmer Creativity

Unleash your full potential on Programmer Netlink. Let your friends, or the world about something. Express through Snippets. Snippets are like posts, but with a fun programming-related term.
